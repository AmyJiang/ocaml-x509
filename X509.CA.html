val sign : signing_request ->
       valid_from:Asn.Time.t ->
       valid_until:Asn.Time.t ->
       ?digest:Nocrypto.Hash.hash ->
       ?serial:Z.t ->
       ?extensions:(bool * X509.Extension.t) list ->
       X509.private_key -> X509.distinguished_name -> X509.t
sign signing_request ~digest ~valid_from ~valid_until ~serial ~extensions private issuer is certificate, a signed certificate. Public key and subject are taken from the signing_request, passed extensions are added to the X.509 certificate. The private key and issuer are used. Digest defaults to `SHA256.
